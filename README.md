(Self-Assessment Survey)
Purpose: This instrument measures the level of disaster preparedness of school personnel in terms of knowledge and awareness, skills and response capacity, attitudes and self-efficacy, communication and coordination, and participation and planning related to common hazards such as fire, floods, typhoons, and earthquakes.
Instruction: Please select the option that best reflects your response using the Likert Scale below.
Scale:
5 – Strongly Agree (SA)
4 – Agree (A)
3 – Neutral (N)
2 – Disagree(D)
1 – Strongly Disagree (SD)
